Favorite Films and TV shows

The Host (괴물)

A Korean masterpiece by Bong Joon-ho that seamlessly blends action, horror, and dark humor while tackling themes of family and societal corruption.

Odio l'estate (I Hate Summer)

My favourite film so far. An Italian comedy-drama that captures the complexities of family dynamics and relationships with wit and relatability. I expected the usual italian comedy, instead I found a comic but very deep movie about the pleasure of life, aging, and how we use to spend time

Seven

A psychological thriller anime that explores morality, power, and the consequences of playing god through an intricate cat-and-mouse game.

Snowpiercer

A dystopic movie underlying the differences of social classes in a society constrained to live in a train.

Shutter Island

A psychological thriller movie, with a great performance of Leonardo Di Caprio.

Chernobyl

An awesome TV series regarding the nuclear incident of Chernobyl.

The office

This TV series was the best way to spend my free time when I had to stay home.

How I met your mother

I love Barney Stinson and Marshall Eriksen, this sitcom is so funny.

Breaking bad

Probably the best TV series.

Game of thrones

Not a good final season, but all the rest is a masterpiece.

The voice of Hind Rajab

A unique experience. This movie tells the real story of a 6 years old Palestinian girl living in a war zone. No dubs and real recordings make this film stunning

Naruto

My favourite shonen anime so far, starting from naruto to naruto shippuden series.

Baccano!

A very interesting and confusing anime that explores the importance of relationships, stories, friendships as the only reasons of life.

Dragonball

A masterpiece of shonen anime. I suggest reading db multiverse manga

Codegeass

One of the most appreciated anime in japan, regarding an eventual war between two big nations

Kiseiju

One the most exciting shonen anime I have ever seen.

Jojo's bizarre adventures

The determination and the values of the characters is something unbelievable. How incredible is Jotaro? How epic and iconic is this whole anime? It is full of references in art, fashion, music.
